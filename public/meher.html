India vs Bangladesh

24/11/2019

The proceedings on Day 3 got wrapped early as Umesh Yadav removed the Bangladesh tail in the first hour of play to hand India an impressive innings and 46 runs victory in their maiden Day/Night Test. In the process, the 32-year-old also completed his fifer. With this victory, India also sealed the series 2-0. This is India’s fourth successive innings win, making them the first team to do so in the history of Test cricket.

India skipper Virat Kohli earlier top scored with 136 before declaring his team’s first innings at 347/9. Kohli reached his 27th Test hundred in the first Day-Night Test played in India and the first pink-ball Test for either team.
